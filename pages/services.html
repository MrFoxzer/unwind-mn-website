What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive holistic care for infants, children, and adults at every stage of life. Rooted in collaboration, compassion, and community.

Craniosacral Therapy & Myofascial Release

Gentle, light-touch therapy that brings balance to all systems of the body. Our practitioners specialize in treating infants through adults, with focused expertise in pregnancy, postpartum recovery, TMJD, traumatic brain injuries, chronic pain, trauma, and injury rehabilitation. We are a tongue-tie friendly provider, offering comprehensive pre- and post-frenectomy support.

Lactation Services

Holistic, evidence-based feeding support backed by over 10 years of experience. We offer both office and home visits to meet you where you are. As a tongue-tie friendly provider, we can combine lactation consultations with bodywork for a comprehensive approach. Breastfeeding classes coming soon to help you prepare for your journey.

Doula Services

Experienced, inclusive, full-spectrum doula support for pregnancy, birth, and postpartum. Our packages can include lactation consultations and bodywork sessions for a holistic experience through every phase. Contact Brittney to learn more about how we can support your unique journey.

Life & Wellness Coaching

Personalized support through life changes, transitions, and personal growth. Our coaches help you cultivate balance, resilience, and well-being. Virtual appointments are available for your convenience. Contact Brittney or Heather to get started on your path forward.
