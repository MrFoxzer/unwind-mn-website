Certification Program

Unwind CST/MFR Certifying Workshops

Alexandria, MN
Starting May 2026
6 Weekends (~1 Year)
Early Bird: $400 / Regular: $450

A comprehensive program designed to develop a deeper understanding of Craniosacral Therapy and Myofascial Release. Over six weekend classes spanning approximately one year, you will learn what CST/MFR is, relevant anatomy, assessment tools, and a full basic and advanced protocol for adults (including pregnant clients), children, and infants.

This program includes ongoing mentorship to support your development as a practitioner. Whether you are a seasoned bodyworker or just beginning your journey, this certification will give you the knowledge and confidence to integrate CST/MFR into your practice.

Alexandria, MN
2026 — Date TBD
